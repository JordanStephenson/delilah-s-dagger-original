@mumasals_tattoos has been part of the furniture here at Delilahs Dagger for a few years. At first it was her obsession with covering as much of her body in art work as possible, then for her love of taking care of/feeding people, and now as one of our shining apprentices.

While on paper she’s a bit older (which we never pass up an opportunity to remind her of) there’s no limit on learning something new. She’s loved to draw and paint her whole life so this feels like the perfect job for her. She is obsessed with all things Disney and that’s her favourite thing to tattoo, but enjoys all characters, animals and flowers. She’s sociable (a chatterbox) loves to make her customers comfortable and welcome, and laughs... a lot and loudly. She’s got lots of interests, including reiki, reading, travelling and yoga, and always has an embarrassing story or two to tell about her kids or her accident prone nature.
