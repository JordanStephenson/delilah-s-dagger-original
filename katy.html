@what.katy.does has been at Delilahs Dagger for almost 4 years. At first Katy specialised in bold black work designs, but has developed an obsession with everything floral (and leopard print but that’s another story!).

She specialises in fine line botanicals and wildlife - especially bees! Once mentioned in the evening standard for her delicate Bee tattoos, she has become well known for tattooing 100s of them on the “bee brigade”. Katy prides her self in making each of her customers feel welcome and secure and will usually be found telling funny stories of her puppy whilst she’s tattooing!
